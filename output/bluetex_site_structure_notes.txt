BLUETEX SHOPIFY HOMEPAGE — STRUCTURE NOTES
Companion document to: bluetex_website_mockup.html
Purpose: Design + structure guide for the new Shopify theme build (not final theme code).

LAYOUT INTENT
-------------
Industrial, squared-off, TermBar-forward homepage modeled on the
ProTapes homepage skeleton. Rebuilt with BlueTex content, real BlueTex
imagery, and the Shopify Concept theme color direction.
"Inspired by ProTapes structure, not copied content."

Design rules enforced in this version:
  - Squared-off: border-radius limited to 2–6px almost everywhere.
    The only 6px radii are on images and the hero carousel frame.
  - No pill CTAs. All buttons are rectangular with 4px corners.
  - No soft SaaS cards. White rectangular content blocks, navy
    bars, orange accent lines, clean dividers.
  - White page body with #f4f4f4 alt-section bands.
  - 1140px fixed container (ProTapes width).
  - #e6e6e6 "label bar" section headings with a 4px navy left bar.
  - Real BlueTex logo image in the header and footer.
  - Real BlueTex TermBar photos throughout.

Color system
  Navy              #12205c   (primary)
  Navy hover        #1a2d80
  Navy deep         #0c1742   (footer, hero visual base)
  Orange            #ee7f12   (accent, CTA highlight, proof accents)
  Orange hover      #c9660a
  Ink               #1b1b1b
  Muted             #5c5c5c
  Hairline          #cbcbcb
  Line soft         #e4e4e7
  Body bg           #ffffff
  Alt band          #f4f4f4
  Label bar bg      #e6e6e6

Typography
  Family: Inter 400/500/600/700/800/900
  Headings: uppercase, 800–900 weight, letter-spacing .2–1.6px
  Body: 15px / 1.55
