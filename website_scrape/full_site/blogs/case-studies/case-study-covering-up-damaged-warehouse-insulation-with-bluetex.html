We hear from a lot of people who are trying to find a solution to cover up their old warehouse insulation that, over time, may have been damaged or just gotten stained and looks unsightly/unprofessional. Did you know BlueTex™ has a cover system application? You can use BlueTex™ to cover over old insulation (without removing it if you don't want to) and it creates a new interior vapor barrier that looks bright and clean! See below for some details from a customer who did just that.

Background: Many facilities are looking for a way to conceal outdated warehouse insulation that has become damaged, discolored, or worn over time—leaving the space looking unattractive and unprofessional. This customer was contracted to cover the bottom wall sections along the warehouse length.

Type of Structure: Red Iron Style Fulfillment Warehouse

Install Date: February 2026

Location: Tennessee

Goal: Cover old insulation without removing it, to create a new interior surface

Product Used: 2mm BlueTex™ Pro 50" Wide

Install Details: To prepare, the contractor moved wall racks away from the walls and created an open area to work in. Because he was not removing the old insulation, the prep work was minimal.

He followed the install instructions on this page for Repairing / Covering Old, Torn Wall Insulation in Metal Buildings Keeping Inside the Wall Girts . He used the existing screws along the floor to spear the BlueTex™ on to, and then secured them in place with washers. He could have covered these with white vapor barrier seam tape, but it's not required.

Finish: The BlueTex™ was installed creating a fresh interior vapor barrier with a clean, bright, professional appearance. This method is great for spaces where the building needs to be presentable for customers or even for employees.

“Everything went well. Looking forward to doing more projects like this.”

Results: The client noticed the immediate improvement in the look of the bottom section of walls and may consider doing the rest of the walls in the future.

That’s a dramatic differenc e — the before/after that the BlueTex™ cover system delivers is unlike no other insulation out there.
