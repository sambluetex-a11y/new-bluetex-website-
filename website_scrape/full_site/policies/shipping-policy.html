Shipping policy

Please use the times shown here and at checkout as an estimate only . If you have an order that is time sensitive, please call us ASAP and we will discuss what options are available. For the latest information on FedEx® operations, please click here .

We offer FREE SHIPPING on all $100+ orders going within the CONUS (48 states). All orders are subject to a shipping fee is the order total is less than $100. If you are in AK or HI, contact us about shipping options: 800-595-8772. Return policy here .

Due to the size and weight of BlueTex™ insulation products, and the unpredictable nature of shipping times, our store does not offer expedited shipping on any orders. If you'd like to arrange a shipper to pick up from our warehouse, you're welcome to do so. Just contact us first to coordinate pick up day/time and send us your labels and we'll package your order and have it ready for your shipper when they arrive!

When will my order ship?

We ship orders out daily, Monday - Friday. Smaller orders will ship the same day when the order is placed by 3pm CST. Orders placed after 3pm or larger orders will ship the following business day. Please note that weather and mechanical delays can sometimes interrupt ship time. If you have a question about timing, please call us: 800-595-8772.

Larger orders going by freight carrier may take 1-4 business days to be picked up; w e have your pallets ready to pick up within 1 business day of your order, but they don’t always get picked up that soon because of carrier shortages and/or delays.

We strongly recommend you do not schedule machinery, equipment, or labor until your shipment has arrived. ETAs can fluctuate and occasionally delays happen. Plan ahead and give yourself extra time to receive your shipment. Past transit times do not guarantee future ones, so don't rely on history for your scheduling.

You will be notified by email (or text if you opted-in at checkout) when your order has shipped.

Will I get a tracking number?

Yes, once your shipment has been prepared for pick up, our system will generate a tracking number and email it to you (using the email you provided at checkout). This email will link you to the carrier we used for your order (USPS, FedEx or LTL carrier) and once you go to the carrier tracking page, you can use your number to see what the ESTIMATED delivery date is. Please note that this date can change/update, depending on numerous factors with the carrier.

If for some reason your order does not arrive on the scheduled delivery date, please check the tracking number on the carrier site first, before contacting us . Usually the reason for delay is minor and it may only affect your delivery by a day. If you cannot find your tracking number, please do an email search or check your spam/junk folders, as that's usually where they end up.

If your tracking has not updated and it's been several days (3+ days past your delivery ETA), feel free to let us know and we can contact the carrier to see if there's a reason for the delay.

What carriers do you ship with?

Depending on the quantity and item, shipping is fulfilled by FedEx Ground, USPS Priority Mail, or by LTL Freight Carrier .

Local customers can contact us to arrange a pick up appointment. Discounts apply to local customers who choose to pick their order up.

How long will it take for your order to arrive?

We ship everything out of our warehouse in North Texas.

The map above is a general representation of transit times for FedEx Ground shipments to commercial destinations . In limited areas, transit times may differ between shipments to commercial and residential destinations. FedEx Ground (commercial zoned property) business days are Monday through Friday (excluding holidays). FedEx Home Delivery (residential zoned property) business days are Tuesday through Saturday (excluding holidays). Example: A package shipped via FedEx Ground on Monday with a transit schedule of 2 business days, (not including the day the package is shipped) would be expected for delivery on Wednesday. A package shipped on Friday with a 2-day transit schedule would be expected for delivery the following Tuesday.

LTL: Freight carrier orders take between 2 and 6 days on average depending on your location.

Do you ship internationally?

At this time we do not ship internationally, but we do ship to all 50 US states. If you have a freight forwarder in the USA, we can work with you to ship your order directly to them, and then you can coordinate forwarding the material from them to your final destination.
