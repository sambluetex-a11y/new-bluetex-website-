Common TermBar Questions View Full FAQ →

Is It Strong?

Yes. TermBars mechanically fasten the material to the building frame. The finished wall is tighter and more durable than the original metal building insulation it covers.

Can You Paint It?

Yes. BlueTex and the TermBars both hold paint. Most customers leave it bright white for light reflection, but you can roll or spray any latex paint for a custom color.

Do I Have to Tear Out the Old Insulation?

Usually no. BlueTex is commonly installed over existing insulation. The TermBar presses the old material in place and the new BlueTex creates the finished interior.
