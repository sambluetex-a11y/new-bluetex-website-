Common Questions View Full FAQ →

Is the $100 really refundable?

It's redeemable, not refunded in cash. Place a follow-on order of $500 or more and we credit the $100 against the new order. One credit per $500 of purchase.

Why 50″ wide and not something wider?

50″ is the width we recommend for metal building cover and repair work — it lines up cleanly with standard purlin and girt spacing and keeps the install fast. The kit ships with 50″ material so what you test on the wall is what you'll install on the full building.

Do I have to tear out the old insulation first?

Usually no. BlueTex is most often installed over the existing insulation. The TermBar presses the old material in place and the new BlueTex creates the finished interior.

Will this work on a red iron building?

Yes. The kit includes a sample set of EasyClips specifically for attaching BlueTex to the roofline on a red iron style building, plus TermBars for the wall sections.

How big a section can I cover with one kit?

One bay. The kit covers roughly 20 ft of 50″-wide material — enough to install one full TermBar run between two purlins or girts so you can see the finished panel look in place.

What's the next step after I test the kit?

Send us photos of your building and the basic dimensions. We'll put together a material list and a quote. Use the $100 from the kit toward that order.

Ready for a Full-Building Material List?

Send photos and rough dimensions of the building. We put together a material list and quote faster when we can see what you're working with — and your $100 sample-box credit goes toward the order.
