ORIGINAL STYLE CONTENT (REFORMATTED FOR REFERENCE)

If you are insulating a metal building, pole barn, airplane hangar, storage facility, garage, or workshop, you are in the right place.

Choosing the right product to fix a problem is important, but having the right process is what makes the product work.

We are building science experts, so we care about getting it right. We are not just here to sell products.

BlueTex offers multiple foil/foam insulation products designed to:

- Block 97% of radiant heat  
- Act as a vapor barrier (depending on product)  
- Provide a clean, finished interior surface  

However, the correct product depends on how the building is used.

--------------------------------------------------
HOW BLUETEX GUIDES CUSTOMERS
--------------------------------------------------

The first questions we ask:

- What is your goal?
- How are you using the building?

Key considerations:

- Are you trying to reduce heat (shade effect)?
- Are you trying to prevent condensation or sweating?
- Will the building be heated or cooled?
- If so, how often?

Types of building usage:

- Non-conditioned (never heated/cooled)
- Semi-conditioned (occasionally heated/cooled)
- Fully conditioned (24/7 controlled environment)

--------------------------------------------------
PRODUCT THINKING (SIMPLIFIED)
--------------------------------------------------

Radiant Barrier (Foil Only):
- Functions as a heat shield
- Reflects radiant heat
- No vapor barrier
- Best for warm climates or attic applications

Thermal Wrap:
- Vapor barrier + radiant barrier
- Designed for pairing with traditional insulation (R-value)
- Best for fully conditioned buildings

2mm Pro:
- Designed for non-conditioned or lightly conditioned buildings
- Works in all climates but most common in warm areas
- Helps with condensation control
- Ideal for limited heating/cooling (approx. under 5–8 hours/week)

6mm Supreme:
- Thicker insulation for increased performance
- Better for colder climates or more frequent heating/cooling
- Helps buildings heat and cool more efficiently
- Best for 8–10+ hours/week usage

--------------------------------------------------
CORE COMPARISON LOGIC
--------------------------------------------------

Both products:
- Block 97% radiant heat
- Act as vapor barriers
- Provide durability and clean interior finish

Differences:

2mm Pro:
- Thinner
- More flexible
- Best for heat reflection and basic use

6mm Supreme:
- Thicker
- Higher insulation value
- Better for colder climates and frequent use

--------------------------------------------------
IMPORTANT ANALOGY USED
--------------------------------------------------

Think of your building like a refrigerator:

- A thicker refrigerator uses more insulation and is more efficient
- A thinner refrigerator requires more energy to maintain temperature

Your building behaves the same way.

--------------------------------------------------
INSTALLATION + USAGE NOTES
--------------------------------------------------

- Can install on roof only for condensation control
- Can combine products (ex: 6mm roof + 2mm walls)
- Full installs (roof + walls) recommended for heat control
