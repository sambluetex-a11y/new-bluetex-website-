    Is Your Insulation Working Against You?
    Sagging & Failing Fiberglass
    Old fiberglass loses its R-value over time, sags under its own weight, and becomes a breeding ground for moisture — accelerating the rot of your structural girts.
    
        
    Condensation "Rain"
    When warm, humid air hits cold fiberglass, it creates condensation that drips onto your equipment, inventory, and floors — just like rain, but from the inside.
    
        
    Bird & Rodent Nests
    Torn and sagging fiberglass is an open invitation for pests. Once they're in, they contaminate your insulation and compromise your entire building envelope.
    
        
    
  
    

    




    


    
  
    
      
    
        
    
          
            
          
        
    
      
    

      
    
        
    Why BlueTex™?
    It's a Vapor Barrier: Stops the condensation.
    It's a Radiant Barrier: Blocks 97% of radiant heat.
    It's a Surface Finish: Gives you a bright, white, washable interior.
