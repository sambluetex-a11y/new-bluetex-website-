Help Me Choose The Right Product

If you are insulating a metal building, pole barn, airplane hangar, storage facility, garage or workshop
you are in the right place. Choosing the right PRODUCT to fix a problem is important, but having the
right PROCESS is what makes the right product work. We are building science experts so we care
about getting it right; we are not just here to sell products.

BlueTex™ offers two popular foil/foam insulation products: the 2mm Pro and the 6mm Supreme.
Both products are designed to block 97% of radiant heat, serve as an effective vapor barrier, and
feature a glossy white interior surface for a clean, finished look. However, they differ in terms of
application and suitability for specific environments.

The chart below gives you some very generic conditions to consider when choosing the right
product. If you have a unique application or aren't sure which approach to take, contact us for more
help.

Heating/Cooling Full Time for a Living Space?

If you're creating a space that will be continuously climate controlled, like an office space or a house, then you need to use the Thermal Wrap product PLUS some R-value insulation.

For this type of application we recommend BlueTex™ Thermal Wrap paired with the R-value of your choice.

Ask Yourself These Questions First

“What’s your goal?”
“How are you using the building?”

- Are you in a hot area and want a shade effect?
- Do you want to stop condensation?
- Will you heat/cool the building?
- How often?

--------------------------------------------------

PRODUCT BREAKDOWN

Radiant Barrier Foil:
- Heat shield
- Reflects radiant heat
- No vapor barrier
- Best for warm climates

Thermal Wrap:
- Vapor barrier + radiant barrier
- Designed to pair with R-value
- Best for fully conditioned buildings

BlueTex™ Pro (2mm):
- For non-conditioned or semi-conditioned buildings
- Best in warm climates
- Occasional heating/cooling (5–8 hrs/week)

BlueTex™ Supreme (6mm):
- 3x thicker
- Better for cold climates
- Regular heating/cooling (8–10+ hrs/week)

--------------------------------------------------

ANALOGY USED

Think of your building like a refrigerator:

- More insulation = more efficiency
- Less insulation = more energy required later

COMMON USE INSIGHT

Most customers choose the 2mm Pro because:

INSTALL NOTES

- Roof-only installs help with condensation
- Full installs help with heat control
- Can combine 2mm + 6mm

FULL CONDITIONED SPACES

If running 24/7 heating/cooling:
- Must combine radiant barrier + R-value
- Thermal Wrap is recommended
